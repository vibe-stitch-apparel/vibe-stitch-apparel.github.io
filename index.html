The Atelier

A distinguished Nigerian fashion house blending international couture standards with the richness of local craftsmanship.

Founded in 2025 and duly incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (RC 875527), Vibe Stitch Apparel Ltd has rapidly become a trusted name for bespoke tailoring, bridal couture, and corporate identity wear. From our studio in Sango Ota, we create garments that transcend trends — each piece is a collaboration between our designers and your unique story, executed with impeccable pattern work, luxurious fabrics, and an unwavering commitment to perfection.
