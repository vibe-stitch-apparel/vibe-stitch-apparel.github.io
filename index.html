About Vibe Stitch

A full-service fashion house blending international design standards with Nigerian craftsmanship. Incorporated on 28th August 2025 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (RC 875527).

We create clothing that carries personality — from one‑off bridal gowns and made‑to‑measure suits to corporate uniforms and limited ready‑to‑wear collections. Every stitch is executed with careful pattern work, fine finishing, and a deep respect for our clients’ stories. We are proudly Nigerian, CAC‑registered, and committed to on‑time delivery.
